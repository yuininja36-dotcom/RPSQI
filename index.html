About Us

The Railway Preservation Society of Queensland is dedicated to preserving and celebrating the rich railway heritage of Queensland. Our mission is to maintain historic locomotives, carriages, and infrastructure for future generations to enjoy and learn from.

We honor the memory of all Queensland railway employees who contributed to building and maintaining the extensive railway network that connected communities across the state.

Our Mission

Preserve

Maintain and restore historic railway equipment and infrastructure

Educate

Share the history and significance of Queensland's railways with the public

Celebrate

Honor the dedication of railway workers throughout Queensland's history
